why the Nigeria jubilee fellows programme?

Other programs and initiatives tend to require some sort of advanced requirement from applicants or lower the entry bar in terms of the kind of opportunities that are available. NJFP does the opposite by inviting qualified applicants across a level playing field without compromising the pedigree of organizations that will play host to successful Fellows. The programme will serve all of Nigeria from North to South and East to West ensuring that organisations and youth from all corners of the country can participate.
